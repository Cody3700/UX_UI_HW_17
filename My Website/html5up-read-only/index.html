About Me

With a lifelong passion for visual art and story telling, I officially started my design career in early 2021 aquiring a solid but diverse range of skills from Berkeley's UX/UI Bootcamp. There I was challenged to undertake many design projects that would serve to highlight my unique set of skills.

When I'm not perfecting my UX/UI work, you'll find me working on my photography, editing audio and video, or enjoying music. I also love to be active, play, and meditate.
